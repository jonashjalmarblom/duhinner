Take Control of Your Time

Despite having more leisure time than ever, most of us feel like time is always running short. Jonas Hjalmar Blom weaves together insights from psychology, neuroscience, and economics to offer a deeper understanding of how we perceive, use, and value our time — and how you can take control of it.

"This is a book for everyone who has ever thought 'I don't have time.'"

Jonas Hjalmar Blom

Jonas Hjalmar Blom is a licensed psychologist, doctoral candidate in psychology, and lecturer with a deep interest in how we relate to time — and why we so rarely feel like we have enough of it.

Through research, books, and lectures, he helps individuals and organisations understand their experience of time, their time culture, and how to create more meaningful ways of living and working.
